Welcome to Our Mission of Change

We are dedicated to educating underprivileged children and uplifting communities through kindness, awareness, and action.

Who We Are

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a non-profit NGO working to provide quality education to children in underserved communities. Our mission is to light the path of learning where it’s needed the most — bastis (slums), rural villages, and forgotten neighborhoods.

What We Do

Free Education Programs

We organize open-air classes and provide learning materials to children with no access to schools.

Food & Nutrition Support

We ensure students receive at least one healthy meal during learning hours.

Digital Literacy

We teach basic computer skills and internet awareness to prepare children for the modern world.

Join Hands to Build a Brighter Future

Every small step creates a ripple of impact. Be a part of our journey to empower lives through education.
