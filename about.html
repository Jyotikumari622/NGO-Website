Who We Are

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a non-profit NGO dedicated to providing free education and social empowerment to children living in slum and underprivileged areas. Founded in 2022, we have been creating change at the grassroots level through informal learning, mentoring, health awareness, and youth development programs.

Our Vision

To build a society where no child is deprived of education, and every community has the power to rise with knowledge, dignity, and equality.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education and life skills to marginalized children
  • Support girl child education and women empowerment
  • Organize health, hygiene, and digital literacy drives
  • Build inclusive and sustainable learning spaces

Our Work in Action

Community Teaching: Street classes, rooftop schools, and community learning centers.

Empowerment Programs: Workshops for youth, parents, and local leaders to promote awareness and engagement.
